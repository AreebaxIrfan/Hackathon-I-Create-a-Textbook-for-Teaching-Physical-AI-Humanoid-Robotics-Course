# Appendices

The Appendices serve as a valuable resource for supplementary information, detailed guides, and troubleshooting tips that complement the core curriculum modules. Here, you will find in-depth technical specifications, setup instructions for various environments, and additional readings to further your understanding and practical skills in Physical AI and Humanoid Robotics.

This section is designed to be a living document, updated periodically to reflect the latest advancements in hardware, software, and cloud robotics platforms. We encourage you to consult these appendices for practical solutions to common challenges encountered during your projects and lab exercises.

## What You Will Find Here

*   **Economy Jetson Kit Details**: Comprehensive technical specifications, advanced setup procedures, and optimization tips for the recommended low-cost Jetson platform.
*   **Cloud Platform Setup Guide**: Step-by-step instructions for configuring popular cloud computing environments (AWS, GCP, Azure, NVIDIA Omniverse Cloud) for scalable robotics simulation and AI model training.
*   **Further Reading and Resources**: A curated list of academic papers, online courses, open-source projects, and communities to deepen your knowledge beyond the scope of this curriculum.
*   **Troubleshooting Guide**: A collection of common issues encountered during ROS 2 development, Isaac Sim usage, and AI model integration, along with their solutions.

The goal of these appendices is to provide you with the practical support needed to successfully navigate the complexities of building autonomous humanoids, ensuring you have access to critical information whenever you need it.
